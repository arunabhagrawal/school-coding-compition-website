Jennifer Pattison Tuohy

Jennifer Pattison Tuohy is Smart Home Reviewer at The Technostan, where she examines all angles of the evolving technology in our homes. She has covered this space since 2013, writing for Wirecutter, Dwell, Wired (U.K.), The Ambient, BBC Science Focus, and US News & World Report, among others. She received her journalism training on London's Fleet Street with The Daily Telegraph before moving to Sun Valley, Idaho, where she worked for local newspapers for ten years. She now lives in Charleston, SC, and in her spare time tends to her flock of chickens, coaches youth tennis, and troubleshoots her smart home.
